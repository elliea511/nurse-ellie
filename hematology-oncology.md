# Hematology / Oncology

Hematology and oncology nursing covers blood disorders, anemias, and cancer care — topics that show up frequently on the NCLEX and ATI exams. This section breaks it down so you can study what you need without wading through everything at once.

<div class="ati-tip">
<strong>Not sure where to start?</strong> Anemias is the best first topic — understanding the different types and how to tell them apart is a high-yield skill that carries over into oncology and beyond.
</div>
