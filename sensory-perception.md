# Sensory Perception

Sensory perception nursing focuses on vision, hearing, neurologic awareness, communication needs, and patient safety when senses are impaired.

<div class="ati-tip"><strong>Study focus:</strong> Think safety first: fall risk, communication barriers, infection precautions, medication effects, and changes in vision or hearing.</div>

## What to review here

- Eye disorders and vision changes
- Ear disorders and hearing changes
- Sensory deficits and safety planning
- Patient teaching for assistive devices
- Priority findings that need follow-up
