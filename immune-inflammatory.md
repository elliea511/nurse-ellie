# Immune & Inflammatory

Immune and inflammatory nursing focuses on how the body protects itself, reacts to injury, responds to infection, and manages inflammation across multiple systems.

<div class="ati-tip"><strong>Study focus:</strong> Connect symptoms to the whole body: fever, swelling, pain, skin changes, allergic response, infection risk, and when inflammation becomes systemic.</div>

## What to review here

- Immune system basics
- Inflammation and infection cues
- Autoimmune and hypersensitivity responses
- Priority safety findings
- Patient teaching for prevention and follow-up
