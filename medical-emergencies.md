# Medical Emergencies

Medical emergencies show up throughout nursing — in the ER, on the floor, in clinicals, and all over the NCLEX. This section breaks them down by topic so you can study the specific emergency you need instead of drowning in one giant list.

<div class="ati-tip">
<strong>Not sure where to start?</strong> Burns is a great first topic — it covers assessment, fluid resuscitation, wound care priorities, and pain management all in one place, and those skills transfer to almost every other emergency category.
</div>
