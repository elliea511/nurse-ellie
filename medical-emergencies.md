# Medical Emergencies

Medical emergencies show up throughout nursing — in the ER, on the floor, in clinicals, and all over the NCLEX. This section breaks them down by category so you can study the specific emergency you need.

<div class="ati-tip">
<strong>Not sure where to start?</strong> Burns is a great first topic — it covers assessment, fluid resuscitation, wound care priorities, and pain management all in one place, and those skills transfer to almost every other emergency category.
</div>
