# Renal & Urinary

Renal and urinary nursing focuses on kidney function, fluid balance, electrolytes, urinary elimination, infection cues, and safety priorities.

<div class="ati-tip"><strong>Study focus:</strong> Watch urine output, creatinine/BUN trends, fluid status, potassium changes, infection signs, and when a change becomes urgent.</div>

## What to review here

- Kidney function and filtration
- Acute and chronic renal problems
- Fluid and electrolyte priorities
- Urinary tract infection cues
- Catheter and urinary elimination safety
