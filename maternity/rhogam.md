# RhoGAM

## Medication

|                 |                        |
| --------------- | ---------------------- |
| Medication Name | RhoGAM                 |
| Generic Name    | Rho(D) immune globulin |
| Category/Class  | Immune globulin        |

## Purpose / Expected Action

* Prevents an Rh-negative mother from forming antibodies against Rh-positive fetal blood.
* Protects future pregnancies from Rh incompatibility complications.
* Prevents Rh sensitization.

## Therapeutic Use

* Given to Rh-negative mothers who may be exposed to Rh-positive fetal blood.
* Helps prevent hemolytic disease in future newborns.

## Route

* IM injection

## Given To

* The mother, not the baby

## Given When

* Around 28 weeks gestation
* Within 72 hours after birth if the newborn is Rh-positive

## Also Given After

* Miscarriage
* Abortion
* Ectopic pregnancy
* Amniocentesis
* Abdominal trauma
* Vaginal bleeding during pregnancy

## Complications / Side Effects

* Injection site soreness
* Mild fever
* Chills
* Rare allergic reaction

## Contraindications / Precautions

Do not give if:

* Mother is Rh-positive
* Mother is already sensitized
* Newborn is Rh-negative postpartum

## Nursing Interventions

* Verify mother is Rh-negative.
* Check antibody screen.
* Verify newborn blood type after birth.
* Give postpartum dose within 72 hours if newborn is Rh-positive.
* Monitor for reaction after administration.
* Document medication, time, site, and patient response.

## Client Education

* This medication is given to the mother.
* It helps prevent problems in future pregnancies.
* It does not treat the current baby’s hemolytic disease.
* It is needed after events where fetal blood may mix with maternal blood.

## Evaluation of Medication Effectiveness

Medication is effective if:

* Mother does not develop Rh antibodies.
* Future pregnancies are protected from Rh sensitization.
* No medication reaction occurs.

## NCLEX / ATI Must Know

RhoGAM is given to the **Rh-negative mother**, not the baby.

It is given within **72 hours postpartum** if the newborn is Rh-positive.

## Memory Hook

**RhoGAM = Rh-negative mom protection.**
