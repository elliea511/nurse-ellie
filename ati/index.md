# ATI Exam Study Guides

Welcome to the ATI Exam Study Guide section. These guides are organized by ATI exam topic and focus on the information most likely to show up in practice questions, rationales, and priority nursing decisions.

These notes are designed to help with:

- Recognizing ATI-style clues
- Understanding priority nursing actions
- Reviewing diet and medication teaching
- Comparing similar conditions
- Studying rationales from practice exams
- Preparing for proctored ATI exams
